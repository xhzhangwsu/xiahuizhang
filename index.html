Biography

Dr. Zhang is a postdoctoral research associate at WSU School of Mechanical and Materials Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Min-Kyu Song. His research interests include controllable synthesis and design of novel nanomaterials such as metal–organic frameworks and their applications in energy storage. He received his Ph.D. degree in Materials Science and Engineering from Washington State University in 2020 (Advisor: Prof. Min-Kyu Song). He received his B.S. degree in Metallurgical Engineering at Central South Univeristy in China in 2011.

Research Interests

  • Synthesis and characterization of metal–organic frameworks (MOFs)
  • Rational design of electrocatalysts for lithium–oxygen batteries
  • MOF-based solid-state electrolytes for lithium–metal batteries
