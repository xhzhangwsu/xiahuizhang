About me

I'm a postdoc in Max Planck Institute for Mathematics working in algebraic geometry. My postdoc mentor is Daniel Huybrechts . I graduated from Tsinghua University in July 2019, under supervision of Eduard Looijenga .

I am interested in K3 surfaces, cubic fourfolds, hyper-Kahler manifolds, Calabi-Yau manifolds. Recently I worked on moduli and automorphisms of those objects.
