Research Interests

  • Synthesis and characterization of metal–organic frameworks (MOFs)
  • Rational design of electrocatalysts for lithium–oxygen batteries
  • MOF-based solid-state electrolytes for lithium–metal batteries
  • Advanced electgrolytes and SEI-forming additives for lithium–metal batteries
